[Budget-Efficient LLM Safety Evaluation & Red-Teaming] 1st Author Passed Round 2
Reframes LLM safety testing as a failure-discovery problem under budget constraints. Proposes efficiency-oriented metrics and a diversity-driven strategy search to enable early discovery of diverse policy violations within fixed query budgets.
  • Proposed efficiency-oriented metrics for discovery timing and harm-category diversity.
  • Evaluated on six frontier LLMs across two safety benchmarks under fixed query budgets.

SILO-BENCH: A Scalable Environment for Evaluating Distributed Coordination in Multi-Agent LLM Systems Accepted
Builds the first multi-agent LLM coordination benchmark grounded in communication complexity theory, revealing that agents spontaneously form appropriate topologies but fail to integrate distributed information—the "communication-reasoning gap."
