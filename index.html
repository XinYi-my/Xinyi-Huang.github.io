About

I am a sophomore at Beijing University of Technology (BJUT), majoring in Information Security (Experimental Class) within the Fan Gongxiu Honors College. My research interests lie at the intersection of multimodal large language models (MLLMs), AI safety, and efficient fine-tuning.

I am currently working on EviGuard, a multimodal safety unlearning framework that introduces evidence-guided connector intervention for cross-modal harmful content suppression in MLLMs, targeting ACM MM 2026. I have also published at ACM MM 2025 and have papers under review at IJCAI ECAI 2026 and ACL ARR 2026.

Beyond research, I serve as study committee member of the Fan Gongxiu Honors College (Class of 2023), have accumulated 100+ hours of volunteer service, and represented BJUT as a student delegate in various national academic forums.
